What is QA Bug Logger?

QA Bug Logger is a Chrome extension that helps QA teams and testers document, track, and manage bugs efficiently. Capture screenshots, annotate issues, and automatically save bug reports to your Google Sheets with screenshots stored in Google Drive.

✓ Verified by Google

Key Features

📸 Screenshot & Annotate

Capture any webpage and use built-in annotation tools to highlight bugs with pen, highlighter, arrows, and rectangles. Make issues crystal clear for your development team.

☁️ Google Drive Integration

Screenshots are automatically uploaded to your Google Drive folder. Organize bug evidence in one centralized, shareable location that your entire team can access.

📊 Google Sheets Logging

Bug details (description, severity, category, device, browser) are automatically saved to your Google Sheet. Track all bugs in a structured, sortable, and filterable format.

🎨 Customizable Categories

Choose from 44+ bug categories including CrossBrowser, Responsive, Layout, API, Performance, and Accessibility issues. Categorize bugs with precision.

⚙️ Flexible Configuration

Use your own Google Sheet and Drive folder. Full control over your data storage. Quick setup with one-click creation of pre-formatted sheets and folders.

🔒 Privacy First

Your data stays in YOUR Google account. We never access, store, or share your bug reports or screenshots. Complete data privacy and ownership.

How It Works

1

Install Extension

Add QA Bug Logger to Chrome from the Web Store. One-click installation.

2

Configure Settings

Set up your Google Sheet and Drive folder, or create new ones with our quick setup tool.

3

Grant Permissions

Authorize access to your Google Sheets and Drive (one-time setup).

4

Log Bugs

Click the extension icon, describe the bug, capture and annotate a screenshot.

5

Auto-Save

Bug details save to your Sheet, screenshot uploads to Drive. Done!

How We Use Your Data

🔐 Your Data, Your Control

QA Bug Logger is designed with privacy as the top priority. Here's exactly what happens with your data:

  • Screenshots: Captured locally and uploaded directly to YOUR Google Drive folder. We never see or store them.
  • Bug Reports: Saved directly to YOUR Google Sheet. We never access or store your bug data.
  • Google Account Access: Only used to upload files to your Drive and append rows to your Sheet. No other Google data is accessed.
  • No Third-Party Servers: All data flows directly from your browser to Google's servers. No intermediary storage.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We don't track your usage, collect analytics, or share any information with third parties.
  • Local Storage Only: Your Sheet and Drive folder preferences are stored locally in Chrome. Never sent to our servers.

🛡️ Privacy Commitment

We request Google Sheets and Google Drive permissions for one purpose only: to save your bug reports to your own Google account. Your data never leaves your control. You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings.
